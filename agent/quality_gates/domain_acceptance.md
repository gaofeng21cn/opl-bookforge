# OPL Book Forge Domain Acceptance Gate

A stage may close for progress with a domain owner receipt, a consumable artifact progress receipt, `completed_with_quality_debt`, a no-output/failure diagnostic, a typed blocker, human gate, or explicit route-back ref. Unresolved review/quality findings, zero output, or corrupt/unreadable output advance as quality debt or a diagnostic; they block domain-ready, quality-accepted, publication-proof, final-export, and production-ready claims. Mechanical completion, schema completeness, provider completion, or generated-surface readiness still cannot declare those claims. Only an unavailable executor, permission/credential failure, explicit human decision, authority violation, irreversible action, or identity/currentness mismatch hard-stops transition.
