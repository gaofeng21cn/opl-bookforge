# OPL Book Forge Domain Acceptance Gate

A stage may close for progress with a domain owner receipt, a consumable artifact progress receipt, `completed_with_quality_debt`, a typed blocker, human gate, or explicit route-back ref. A consumable artifact with unresolved review/quality findings advances; its quality debt blocks domain-ready, quality-accepted, publication-proof, final-export, and production-ready claims. Mechanical completion, schema completeness, provider completion, or generated-surface readiness still cannot declare those claims. Only zero consumable artifact, corrupt/unreadable artifact, permission/credential failure, explicit human decision, authority violation, or identity/currentness mismatch hard-stops transition.
