# OPL Book Forge Domain Acceptance Gate

A stage may close for progress with a domain owner receipt, a consumable artifact progress receipt, `completed_with_quality_debt`, a materialized exact-ref-and-hash no-output/failure diagnostic, a typed blocker, human gate, or explicit route-back ref. Unresolved review/quality findings, missing intended output, or corrupt/unreadable intended output may advance as quality debt or a diagnostic only when an exact consumable artifact or diagnostic exists; they block domain-ready, quality-accepted, publication-proof, final-export, and production-ready claims. Literal zero consumable artifact is a controller hard stop and produces no route decision or recommendation. Mechanical completion, schema completeness, provider completion, or generated-surface readiness still cannot declare stronger claims. An unavailable executor, permission/credential failure, explicit human decision, authority violation, irreversible action, identity/currentness mismatch, or literal zero consumable artifact hard-stops transition.
