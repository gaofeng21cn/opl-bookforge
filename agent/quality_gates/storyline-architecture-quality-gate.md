# Storyline Architecture Quality Gate

Quality gate declaration is required for every generated OPL-compatible stage.
Dedicated review stage is conditional: create one when storyline decisions change owner intent, chapter ordering, evidence scope, publication promise, or editorial position.

Pass conditions:

- The premise, audience promise, reader-style contract, chapter thesis chain, evidence map, and style contract are explicit refs.
- The reader-style contract names primary readers, secondary readers, excluded readers, reading situation, reader anxieties, prior knowledge assumptions, natural-expression rules, forbidden voice patterns, and owner-review status.
- The reader-style contract states whether secondary readers are co-primary, read-along only, or excluded from voice decisions; secondary readers must not silently create separate chapter obligations, lower-density exposition, or a competing voice target.
- Each chapter has a role in the book-level arc and a clear reason to exist.
- Source gaps, unsupported claims, owner decisions, and sequencing risks are listed as direct evidence refs or typed blockers.
- The proposed voice uses affirmative, specific, human prose and avoids generic AI-sounding transitions.
- The next stage receives stable structure for chapter drafting, illustration/table planning, and layout decisions.

Quality-debt and claim-closed conditions (never stage-progression blockers while a readable artifact exists):

- Missing source corpus, audience, owner intent, publication form, or style ref.
- Audience or natural-expression style is guessed generically when source refs do not support a high-confidence inference and no owner question/blocker is recorded.
- Secondary readers are treated as hidden co-primary readers, difficulty downgrades, or density-lowering obligations without owner approval.
- Missing reader-style contract before body drafting or chapter expansion.
- Chapter plan is a topic list without argument movement.
- Review relies on shared execution context instead of direct evidence refs.
- Any generated surface tries to claim final book quality, export readiness, or publication approval.

Codex may carry the best storyline draft into any declared stage or route back. Only zero/corrupt output, authority, permission/safety, identity/currentness, irreversible action, or explicit human authority hard-stops progression.
